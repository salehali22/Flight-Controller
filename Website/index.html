An open-source 40.5 × 40.5 mm six-layer flight controller bridging FPV racing boards and full autopilots — dual IMUs, twelve DMA-backed PWM outputs, and verified flight on Betaflight, iNAV, and ArduPilot.

About

Project Overview

Designed, manufactured, and flight-tested at Ilia State University — from KiCad schematics and JLCPCB fabrication to stable hover on 5-inch and 10-inch platforms.

🎯

Objective

Build SAL FC — a compact open-source controller that fits 30.5 mm FPV mounts while delivering autopilot-class interfaces and dual-IMU redundancy.

🔧

Approach

Hardware-first design in KiCad, hand assembly, then custom target files and HAL patches for Betaflight 4.5.1, iNAV 9.0.1, and ArduPilot.

⚡

Technology

STM32H743VIT6 @ 480 MHz, ICM42688P + BMI270, BMP388, W25Q128 flash, AT7456E OSD, CAN, and twelve DMA-backed PWM outputs.

✅

Outcome

Power rails, DSHOT600, sensors, GPS, and CRSF verified on the bench; stable hover demonstrated; design files released for educational reuse.

01

FPV Size, Autopilot IO

Seven UARTs, four SPI buses, three I²C buses, CAN, USB-C host/device, and DJI Air Unit support on a 40.5 × 40.5 mm board.

02

Dissimilar Dual IMUs

ICM42688P and BMI270 on separate SPI buses, following Pixhawk FMUv6C guidance to reduce common-mode sensor failure risk.

03

Six-Layer EMC Stack-Up

JLCPCB JLC06161H-3313 stack-up with dedicated grounds, ENIG finish, and resin-filled vias per IPC-2221 / IPC-2222 practice.

04

Three Firmware Stacks

Custom targets from source for Betaflight, iNAV (including an SPI6 HAL patch), and ArduPilot with Mission Planner / MAVLink validation.

Technical

Specifications

Hardware specifications of the manufactured SAL FC board.

Processing & Control

Core
  • Microcontroller STM32H743VIT6
  • CPU Core ARM Cortex-M7 @ 480 MHz
  • FPU Single-precision floating-point
  • RAM 1 MB on-chip
  • Flash 2 MB on-chip + W25Q128 SPI
  • Clock Source External HSE crystal oscillator

Sensors & Peripherals

Sensing
  • Primary IMU ICM42688P (SPI)
  • Secondary IMU BMI270 (SPI)
  • Barometric BMP388 (I²C)
  • OSD AT7456E
  • GPS / Mag External UART + I²C
  • Motor Protocol DSHOT600 verified

Communication Interfaces

I/O
  • PWM Outputs 12 channels (dedicated DMA)
  • UART 7 ports
  • SPI 4 buses
  • I²C 3 buses
  • CAN Bus SN65HVD230 transceiver
  • USB USB-C host + device

Power & PCB

Hardware
  • PCB Layers 6-layer (JLCPCB)
  • Dimensions 40.5 × 40.5 mm
  • Mounting 30.5 mm M3 pattern
  • Input Voltage Up to 6S (25.2 V)
  • Regulation 5 V / 8.5 V bucks + 3.3 V LDOs
  • Video Analog OSD + DJI Air Unit
